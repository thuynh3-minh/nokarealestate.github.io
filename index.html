About us

Build a great and innovative company by partnering with the city’s government and bring in affordable housing without the need of new construction. At the same time preserve the historic heart of downtown San Angelo and bring new life in through innovation and collaboration. By taking on abandoned buildings, we take on new challenges that nobody wants to take on.

Noka Real Estate gives us the best housing option. My wife, two kids and three dogs are all satisfied.

Our mission is to create a franchisable model to alleviate residential housing market shortages and the affordable housing crisis. Convert dilapidated vacant downtown buildings to livable spaces thus bolstering downtown structure economy.
